Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our transition to a private platform

When exactly does this change take effect?

The transition to a members-only platform takes effect on January 1, 2026. After this date, you'll need an account to access GPL Times.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by making any purchase on our site. Your account will be automatically created during the checkout process.

Will my existing downloads still work?

Yes, any items you've already downloaded will continue to work. This change only affects accessing new content from our website.

Is there a registration fee?

No, there's no separate registration fee. Accounts are created automatically when you make your first purchase on GPL Times.

What happens if I miss the deadline?

After December 31, 2025, new account registration will be closed. However, we may have special limited reopenings during events like Black Friday.

Will prices change for members?

Our pricing structure remains the same for existing members. You'll continue to enjoy the same great value with enhanced benefits.
